Our Story

Built on Craft.
Scaled by Trust.

Founded in 2008 in Shandong Province, HD Glass (Houde Glassware Co., Ltd.) has grown from a regional manufacturer into a globally-recognised OEM/ODM glassware supplier.

Our 50,000 m² facility runs fully-automated production lines capable of handling orders from 500 to 5,000,000 pieces — maintaining the same exacting quality at every scale.

  • ISO-certified quality management system
  • Solar-powered eco-friendly production
  • Dedicated R&D team for custom mould design
  • Australia-based sales representative (Perth, WA)
